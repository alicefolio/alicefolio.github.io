Global COVID Deaths 2020-2021

This interactive dashboard, powered by Tableau, tracks Covid-19-related deaths between January 2020 and April 2021. The dataset includes 59 columns and was shared by YouTube user AlexTheAnalyst

Problem Statement

  • Total number of cases and deaths.
  • Which Continet has the highest rate of infection?
  • Which Continet has the highest death count?
  • The trend of the infection.

Visualisation and Insight

The visualisation can show where in the world COVID-19 has hit the hardest, which can help with planning and distributing resources effectively. Also, it could be used to monitor the rollout of vaccination programs and assess their effectiveness in reducing the incidence of COVID-19 cases, deaths, and overall population infection rates.

Global Number

Identifying Global Hotspots

Tracking Trends

The infection rates in the UK, US, and Canada has sharply risen between Q1 and Q3 in 2021, with the figures indicating that they has doubled within six months.
