Human Resources Analysis

This interactive dashboard, powered by Power BI, tracks overall employee satifisation rate, attrition rate and salary range. The dataset includes 38 columns and was shared by Kaggle@KAKISAMA

Problem Statement

  • Which department has the highest attrition rate?
  • What is the average employee satisfaction rating?
  • What is the average salary for employees with different levels of experience?
  • What is the gender pay gap?
  • Where the company can improve by satisfaction scores?

Visualisation and Insight

A dashboard about Human Resources that contains turnover rate, satisfaction scores, salary by year of services and level of education by field could reveal several valuable insights about an organization's workforce.

Attrition Rate

Overall the attrition rate is 7.13%. The Research and Development is the highest percentage, which is 7.17%

Employee Satisfaction

Employee satisfaction is currently hovering around a 2.75 on average. This is below the industry standard of 3.0, indicating that there is room for improvement. The environment and work-life balance are the two areas with the lowest satisfaction ratings, both scoring at a 1.

Average Salary by Year of Services
  • 0-4 year: 155,605
  • 5-9 year: 53.994
  • 10-14 year: 15,250
  • 14+ year: 29,684
Gender Pay Gap

Overall the rate is 8.85%. And the range is between 11.96% and 45.13%.

Area of Improvement for the Attrition Rate

On average valuable employees that leave are not satisfayed, the satifisation of work life balance is under score, it is showing that they spent too much times in the company and business trips.
