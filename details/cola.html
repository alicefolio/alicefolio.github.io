Visualisation and Insight

This sales dashboard is a visual representation of sales metrics that provides an “at a glance” look at progress toward sales goals. Some figures that you might want to consider including in your sales dashboard are the total sales revenue generated over a specific period of time, that can help you identify trends in sales performance, and the average price sold, which can help you identify changes in pricing strategy.
